About the Featured Projects

Welcome to our Featured Projects page! This collection of projects represents work being done in a variety of humanities and social sciences departments at Carolina. The projects featured here represent how digital methods can be used to advance the study and teaching of the American South. These methods range from GIS mapping to podcasting to text analysis, resulting in an intellectually rich array of projects.

The projects featured here have been created in the last 5 years or so, with most being from the past year or two. They demonstrate that Carolina is rich with digital pedagogy initiatives that foster collaboration among faculty, staff, and students of all standing.
