About the Featured Collections

The Louis Round Wilson Library houses the special collections at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. It houses the University Archives and Records Management Services and five collections: the North Carolina Collection, the Rare Book Collection, the Southern Folklife Collection, and the Southern Historical Collection. These collections are available for research and classroom use. The Libraries has digitized many collections; a list of fully digitized collections can be found here alongside some notable digital exhibits.

The collections included here are either completely or partially digitized, and have been either used for digital scholarship projects or are being featured here to serve as inspiration for future research on the South.
